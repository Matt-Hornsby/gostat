    Empirical CDF


    Credible interval for a sample from a posterior density

    Empirical quantile from a sample from a PDF | PMF.

    Parameterizing a Gamma distribution by mode and sd. It is more intuitive
    to start with the mode and standard deviation, instead of the mean and
    standard deviation as used in the Kruschke (2011) book. The reason is
    that the gamma distribution is typically very skewed, and therefore the
    location of the mean is not very intuitive. This function computes the
    shape and rate parameters of the gamma distribution from a desired mode
    and standard deviation. After
    http://doingbayesiandataanalysis.blogspot.com/2012/01/parameterizing-gamma-distribution-by.html

    Probability of a one sided null hypothesis from a sample from a
    posterior density.
